ERSCI

Urban furniture research

L'École de Design / 2018

ERSCI seek to make mindful of the advantages offered by the concept of eco-districts to the inhabitants of a concrete district.
This project takes on its full meaning if it considered in a particular location in Nantes, the square Maquis-de-Saffré. After a reflexion on the differents possibilities concerning the renewable energies in urban area, I looked into the knowing of the subject by the citizens, wich is very poor actually. Energy saving of course, but also social connections are notions tackled by this concept of the city of tomorow. And in this neighbouhood full of activity, what better than a public area where neighbours can meet themselves ?
So this urban furtiture, thought of as an event, has as purpose to be a meeting area for neighbours and a information media about renewable energies in the city.
